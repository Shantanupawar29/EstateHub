EstateHub Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Data We Collect

  • Personal info: name, email, phone (for listings/contacts).
  • Property data: addresses, prices, photos.
  • Usage: IP, device, browsing behavior (anonymized analytics).

2. How We Use Data (GDPR Compliant)

  • Provide services (match buyers/sellers).
  • CRM: Feedback analysis, personalized emails (opt-out available).
  • Security: Fraud detection via HTTPS/JWT.
  • No selling data to third parties.

3. Data Protection

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Password hashing (bcrypt), secure storage (MongoDB).
  • Retention: Delete inactive listings after 6 months.

4. Your Rights

  • Access/delete data: Email support@estatehub.com.
  • GDPR: Consent withdrawal, data portability.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies for login/session. Analytics cookies optional (opt-out).
